How to Play

Poker Dice uses 5 dice marked with playing card faces: A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9 — Ace being highest.

Your Turn

You get up to 3 rolls. After your first roll, tap any dice you want to keep — they'll be held and won't re-roll. When you're happy with your hand (or out of rolls), bank it. Try to get the best hand.

Game Modes
Classic Mode
Roll up to 3 times — best hand wins. For when you don't have dice on you. Great for playing with friends in the pub.
Score Mode
5 rounds each. Bank your best hand each turn. Highest total points at the end wins.
Betting Mode
Everyone starts with $5. Each round: place your bets, then roll. Best hand takes the pot. Last player standing wins.
Wild Mode
Same as Betting, but every turn starts with a roll of the Wild Die — a random twist that shakes things up.
